038games “powered by Windesheim and Cibap” has organized the Global Game Jam 2017, 2018 and 2019 in Zwolle. The Global Game Jam is an international Jam-event.

The goal of the event is to bring people together to design and build a game in 48 hours with teams of people who may never worked together.  In 2017 more then 40.000 jammers worldwide attended. In the Netherlands more the 900 jammers where busy.

The event will be hosted by Cibap in it’s “Ontwerpfabriek” (Design Factory)
